Oasis Church Reading

Christ-centred. Inclusive. We belong to each other.

More About Us

Oasis Reading is an inclusive and progressive church and is part of the Oasis Church Network whose vision is for community – a place where everyone is included, makes a contribution, realises a deep sense of belonging and reaches their God-given potential.

More about us Gatherings

What We Do

We meet all together on Sundays plus we have a weeknight gathering. We’re pretty new and we have some exciting community projects in the pipeline…

What We Believe

As an Oasis church, we hold an open, progressive theology. That means we value the love of God, the inclusion of all, the ongoing transformation of self and the world around us. Find out more by clicking on ‘More About Us' below.

Join in

We’d love you to get in touch with us if you are interested in joining in with anything we do. You are so welcome. There are ways to come and ‘just be’ or get busy doing some good stuff that positively impacts our community. Whatever season you’re in, come say hi!

Find us

We currently hire community space to meet on Sundays in the Earley/University area.

Our mid-week gatherings are local homes or favourite public houses.

Every few weeks we meet at Rushall Farm in Bradfield to breath the free air.
